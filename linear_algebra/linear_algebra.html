linear_algebra

linear_algebra, Fortran90 codes which carry out various linear algebra operations for matrices stored in a variety of formats.

The formats include many defined by the LINPACK and LAPACK packages and various sparse formats.

For a given matrix format, the operations supplied may include

  • Conversion to or from general rectangular format (GE).
  • Determinant.
  • Inverse.
  • Matrix-matrix multiplication.
  • Matrix-vector multiplication.
  • Solution of linear systems by Conjugate Gradient (CG) method.
  • Solution of linear systems by Gauss elimination.
  • Transpose.
