toms453

toms453, a FORTRAN90 code which implements ACM TOMS algorithm 453, which computes the abscissas and weights of a Gaussian quadrature formula of given order for Bromwich's integral.

The Bromwich integral is the inverse of the Laplace transform.

The text of many ACM TOMS algorithms is available online through ACM: https://calgo.acm.org/ or NETLIB: https://www.netlib.org/toms/index.html.

Usage: 


        call bromin ( n, s, tol, xr, xi, wr, wi, eps, ier )
N
the order of the rule;
S
the parameter in the integral;
TOL
an error tolerance;
XR, XI
the real and imaginary parts of the abscissas;
WR, WI
the real and imaginary parts of the weights;
EPS
the relatve accuracy estimate;
IER
the error flag.
