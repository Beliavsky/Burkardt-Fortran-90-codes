quadrilateral_witherden_rule

quadrilateral_witherden_rule, a MATLAB code which returns a Witherden quadrature rule, with exactness up to total degree 21, over the interior of a quadrilateral.

The unit quadrilateral has vertices (0,0), (1,0), (1,1), (0,1).
